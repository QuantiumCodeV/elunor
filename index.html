Elunor — Your Personal AI Trading Bot

It connects to a user's Solana wallet, analyzes their trading history and token interactions, identifies patterns and mistakes, and helps build a more resilient strategy.
How it works
Wallet Connection

The user connects a Solana wallet via a private key. This enables deep analysis of all historical trades and behaviors.

Behavioral Analysis

Entry/exit timing, trade rhythm, emotional triggers Recurring mistakes, impulsiveness, lack of planning.

Feedback Loop

Elunor generates a report that highlights mistakes: late entries, toxic tokens, poor exits, no stop-loss.

Wallet Connection

For every token traded, Elunor checks: Project activity and reputation across Twitter and Telegram (via parsing) Website availability, structure, design templates. On-chain behavior: sniper activity, contract reuse, liquidity injection patterns.

FAQ
What is Elunor?
An intelligent Telegram bot that analyzes your trading behavior, identifies your mistakes, and evolves to trade better than you do — based on your own data
How is it different from other bots?
It doesn't copy other traders or push external signals. Elunor learns from your own trades, creating a strategy rooted in your habits — minus the flaws.
How does it detect bad tokens?
By parsing Twitter, Telegram, and project websites. It checks reputation, contract history, bot activity, and previous relaunch patterns.
Does it really trade on its own?
Yes. Once the system learns your style, you can activate auto-trade. Elunor will enter and exit positions on its own — and explain every decision.
Is connecting a wallet safe?
A private key is required for trade analysis and automation. Elunor never shares or stores keys externally — all processing is local to the system.
DWhat about safety?
KOYA checks contract mint functions, renounce status, LP lock, sniper wallets, and abnormal bot or wallet activity.
